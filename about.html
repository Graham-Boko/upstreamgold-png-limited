About Us

Driving sustainable growth and national development in Papua New Guinea

Company Overview


Upstream Gold (PNG) Ltd is a nationally owned company engaged in mining, petroleum, logistics and development services. With a strong vision for sustainable development, the company aims to be a strategic leader and trusted partner in Papua New Guinea’s extractive industry.

Mission & Vision


Mission


To create and establish strategically positioned businesses, socially responsible programs and practical solutions that lay the foundation for long-term development.


Vision


To promote, enhance and advance service delivery through dynamic development models for growth and prosperity.
